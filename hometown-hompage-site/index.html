Top three activities to do in India

Visit UNESCO World Heritage Sites

As of 2022, there are 40 World Heritage Sites located in India. Out of these, 32 are cultural, 7 are natural, and one, the Khangchendzonga National Park, is of mixed type. India has the sixth largest number of sites in the world. The first sites to be listed were the Ajanta Caves, Ellora Caves, Agra Fort, and Taj Mahal, all of which were inscribed in the 1983 session of the World Heritage Committee.

Discover Rich Culture

Make a cultural call in India by unfolding the glorious pages of the golden past. Reminisce the times of yore in the province which stands on the foundation of ancient cultural heritage. The country has its roots deeply ensconced in the past and its legacy has been passed on from generation to generation. Probe into the grandiosity, patterned for crowned heads with a heady mix of contemporariness and be a part of warm traditions!

Enjoy Different Authentic Indian cuisines

Indian cuisine is part of the landscape in most cities across the world. Indian dishes aren’t just curries, chutney, and chicken tikka masala, there’s a lot more to Indian food than you ever knew!
